RapidRush Rafting

At RapidRush Rafting, our mission is to connect people with the raw power and beauty of nature through safe, exhilarating white water adventures. We believe every river tells a story — and we're here to help you live it. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned paddler, we guide you every stroke of the way.

History

RapidRush Rafting was founded in 1998 by a group of passionate river guides who dreamed of sharing their love for white water with the world. What started as a small operation with two rafts and a pickup truck has grown into one of the region's most trusted outdoor adventure companies. Over the decades, we have guided thousands of guests through some of the most breathtaking river corridors in the country, always putting safety, sustainability, and fun at the heart of everything we do.
